About JoinHub

What is JoinHub?

JoinHub is your gateway to the WVSU community — a platform where students can explore, apply to, and connect with organizations that align with their interests.

Whether you're passionate about academics, arts, sports, or community service, JoinHub makes it easy to find organizations that match your goals. Connect with like-minded peers, stay updated on events, and build meaningful relationships that will last throughout your university journey.
